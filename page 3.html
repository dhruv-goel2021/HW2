About Us

Founded in 2020, Sunnies R Us was the brain child of Dhruv Goel. Living in Texas during the Covid-19 pandemic, Dhruv learned the importance of proper eye protection during his many outdoor walks. After about 90 days of cabin fever, he decided that unprecedented times call for unprecedented decisions. He quit his job, found a manufacturer in his hometown, and designed his first prototype. Twelve months and millions in sales later we are now the premiere product in this industry.
